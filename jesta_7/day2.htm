➛•Rapti Engineering College (REC) is one of the well-known private technical colleges in western Nepal, located in Ghorahi-16, Sarra, Dang. It was established in 2008 with the goal of providing engineering and IT education outside Kathmandu. ◉Main things about REC Affiliated with Pokhara University and Council for Technical Education and Vocational Training (CTEVT). Offers bachelor programs like: •BE Civil •BE Computer •Electronics & Communication •BCA •Also offers diploma and pre-diploma engineering courses. Facilities According to the college and education portals, REC has: •Computer labs •Library and e-resources •Internship support •Clubs and extracurricular activities •Technical events and quiz competitions Reputation ➛•In Dang and the Mid-Western/Lumbini region, REC is considered one of the established engineering colleges. Many students from rural districts study there because it is more affordable and accessible than Kathmandu colleges.
